Book Reading App

Learning Portfolio by Ronnie Noche

This Book Reading App will read any texts pointed at the camera as long as it is written in English.
This will be soon updated to cater to Filipino language.

Instructions:
1. Point a book or any item with written words to your device's camera. Make sure the camera focuses on the letters.
2. In your keyboard, press the spacebar and wait for the magic to happen

NOTE: Before starting, make sure your device allow access to the camera.
